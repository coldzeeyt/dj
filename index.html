About

Brad Savage — known on the decks as DJ Scratchy Pants — has been a fixture in the scene since 2002, cutting records before most people knew what a CDJ was. What started as a relentless passion for vinyl evolved into a full career on the airwaves, where he now holds down his own slot at The Summit FM. Whether it's a packed club night, a private event, or a live broadcast, Scratchy Pants brings two decades of instinct, precision, and raw energy to every set.
